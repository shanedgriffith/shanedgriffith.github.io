I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Georgia Tech under Prof. Cedric Pradalier in the DREAM lab. Shout out to Supelec and MaLIS, with whom I am affiliated. Before coming here I worked under Prof. Andrea Thomaz and Prof. Charles Isbell in the Interactive Machine Learning Lab and at Bosch in the Autonomous Driving group. I received my M.S. from Iowa State University in 2011 under Prof. Alexander Stoytchev in the Developmental Robotics Lab.

I invest my free time in a small number of things. I am often immersed in psychology: reading, theorizing, and problem solving on cognition. I want to know how it all fits together in one coherent story. Non-mind, I lift regularly to be fit and make gains. I also aspire to write a book of my own.
