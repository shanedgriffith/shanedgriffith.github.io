I am a 3D computer vision scientist, currently at TuSimple. I have expertise in 3D computer vision, robotics, and machine learning. I have been part of multiple academic labs and internships (see LinkedIn). I obtained my Ph.D. from the Georgia Institute of Technology in Fall 2019. I received my M.S. and B.S. in 2011 and 2008, respectively from Iowa State University.
